Who is Pat?

He's helpful, athletic, driven and perhaps a little too restless. But what'd you expect from somebody with his position.

He was born in an average family in an average port. He lived without worry until he was about 12 years old, but at that point things changed.

He studied a lot and was learning how to cook in new styles. Alongside trusted friends, he managed to thrive in a weird world. But with his honesty and skills, there's nothing to stop him from improving the world. He could quickly become an unstoppable force.

Despite all this success, he is currently still looking for a true calling. He feels like there's more than what we get to know in this world. Luckily he has plenty of resources to support him..
